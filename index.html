Prep Instructions
  1. Obtain darbepoetin (Aranesp) vial — — mcg/mL vial — from IDS Room SP1004, Refrigerator #4.
  2. Withdraw ___ mL (Box C) into an appropriately sized syringe, including overfill.
  3. Place a syringe cap on the syringe.
  4. Do not shake. Protect from light (dispense in amber bag).
  5. BUD: 24 hours refrigerated. Store doses in refrigerator until administration.
  6. Save vials for IDS.
